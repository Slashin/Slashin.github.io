This was by far the most complicated game that I have ever experimented with. There was a lot of logic involved in creating this game mostly due to the difficulty associated with some of the simple tasks (eating food, movement, collision detection and avoidance etc.). However, I felt it was an amazing learning experience, and even though the game is still kind of glitchy, it is definitely quite functional. I plan on making it smoother soon.
