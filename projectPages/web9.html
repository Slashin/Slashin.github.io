Piezoelectric Vibration-Sensing Shoe

Previous Next

I have been working on a project for the past 2 years and have come a long way with it in terms of design, innovation and presentation. The Piezoelectric Vibration-Sensing Shoe is a device that I created to help those who are audibly and/or visually impaired to better navigate their surroundings. With the use of a Arduino Uno microcontroller and many other electronic components, I was able to design a software program that receives vibratory signals from the sensor and notifies the user through a buzzing sensation from a vibration motor on the user's ankle region. There is a lot to explain so please view my "5 Page PDF report", the different websites provided in the links below!

Tools Used: Arduino, C, Adafruit, Electrical Components, Soldering
